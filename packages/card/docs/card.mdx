## Usage

### UiCard

<Playground>
  <UiCard>
    <UiHeading>Card header</UiHeading>
    <UiText>This is the content</UiText>
    <UiText size="xsmall">This is the footer</UiText>
  </UiCard>
</Playground>

### Card as a link

<Playground>
  <UiCard link="https://www.uireact.io" bordered>
    <UiText>Open UiReact docs</UiText>
  </UiCard>
</Playground>

### UiCard with click handler

<Playground>
  <UiCard clickHandler={(identifier) => console.log(`Card ${identifier} clicked`)} identifier="My-ID" squared>
    <UiHeading>Click me!</UiHeading>
  </UiCard>
</Playground>
